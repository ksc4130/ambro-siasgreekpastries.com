Catering

Ambro-Sia’s offers authentic, gourmet, delicious, and delectable Greek cookies and pastries for either small gatherings or large formal events. All pastries are placed in individual greaseproof paper cups--except for koulourakia and paximadia (“Greek biscotti”). The trays will be sealed with clear wrap and covered with lids or wrapped in cellophane and tied with your choice of beautiful wide ribbon. We will prepare, deliver, and setup your dessert table for an additional charge.

A standard delivery and set up fee of $35.00 is applied within a 30-miles radius plus charges for the disposable catering trays; however, the trays are yours to keep. Delivery charges may vary based on location. For small gatherings you may pick up your order, if you prefer. To place your order, please send us your inquiry via the contact form or call us at 614-855-5954. A member of our team will be happy to discuss your specific needs. After discussing your event and an order is placed, you will receive an invoice via email, fax, or regular mail.

We take pride in serving our delicious gourmet cookies and pastries for events such as:

  • Anniversaries
  • Birthdays
  • Celebrations of Life
  • Christenings
  • Graduations
  • Holidays
  • Home Parties
  • Corporate Meetings
  • Retirement Parties
  • Reunions
  • Showers
  • Wedding Events

Payment is required at least one week prior to the event.

To ensure quality service, we will contact your venue and/or your event coordinator for delivery instructions and setup procedures; however, we will not be responsible for the décor of the dessert table. We will make suggestions on the number of tables needed for a beautiful display, and we will verify the number of trays delivered. We require signature upon receipt. The trays will be sealed with clear wrap and a lid or wrapped with cellophane and beautiful bows, whichever you prefer.

Most of the pastries and cookies are offered in a variety of sizes for your specific needs. To help determine the needs for the success of your event, you may wish to consider the number of desserts you would like to offer your guests, their tastes (or special dietary needs), your budget, and most importantly, your own comfort level.
